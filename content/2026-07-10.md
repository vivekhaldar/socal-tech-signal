# SoCal Tech Signal

**Pilot edition · July 10, 2026**<br>
**Events from Saturday, July 11 through Tuesday, July 21**<br>
AI, engineering, security, and founder events across Orange County, Los Angeles, and San Diego.

Southern California has a real builder week ahead. LA has the deepest technical slate, including Claude Code, defense cybersecurity, and a two-day spatial-AI hackathon. OC is strongest on practical learning—vibe coding, quantum computing, and AI operator workflows—while San Diego has an unusually good run of small, focused developer communities.

This expanded edition uses one quality threshold across the region. There is no total-event cap and no requirement that OC, LA, and San Diego have equal counts.

## How this issue was made

Candidates were collected from the current Luma LA and San Diego discovery pages, Meetup, Eventbrite, Builders & Backers, SoCalCyber, The Ticket 500, dev.events, and targeted organizer searches. The expanded edition contains 33 qualifying event occurrences in the primary July 11–21 window—10 in Orange County, 12 in Los Angeles, and 11 in San Diego—plus 4 high-signal events in “Later on the radar.” Every selected date, time, city, and registration state was checked against a canonical event or organizer page on July 10, 2026 between roughly 1:50 and 2:15 p.m. PT.

The filter deliberately rejected generic networking lists, virtual-only events presented as local events, stale/past listings, duplicated platform copies, and listings with unresolved date or location conflicts. Event details can still change; check the linked registration page before traveling.
