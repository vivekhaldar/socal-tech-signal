# SoCal Tech Signal

**Edition · July 19, 2026**<br>
**Events from Sunday, July 19 through Sunday, August 2**<br>
AI, engineering, security, and founder events across Orange County, Los Angeles, and San Diego.

The clearest signal in this issue is that AI has moved from prompting into systems work. Across the region, the best events focus on coding agents, security boundaries, production architecture, automation, and hands-on building; there is also a particularly strong run of applied workshops for operators, public-sector teams, and mission-driven organizations.

This edition uses one quality threshold across the region. There is no total-event cap and no requirement that Orange County, Los Angeles, and San Diego have equal counts.

## How this issue was made

Candidates were collected from the prior issue, Luma’s Los Angeles and San Diego discovery pages, Meetup, Eventbrite, Builders & Backers, The Ticket 500, SoCalCyber, dev.events, InfosecMap, recurring organizer and professional-chapter calendars, university and accelerator searches, and a fresh open-web sweep. A read-only project-inbox search found subscription confirmations but no inbound event submissions. Nine prior-issue events were carried forward because they were still upcoming or in progress at the refresh cutoff.

This issue contains 42 qualifying event occurrences from July 19 through August 2: 16 in Orange County, 14 in Los Angeles, and 12 in San Diego. Every selected date, time, city, in-person status, and visible access condition was checked against a canonical event or official organizer page on July 19, 2026 between roughly 10:00 a.m. and 12:38 p.m. PT.

The filter rejected generic networking lists, virtual-only events presented as local, stale or duplicated listings, thin lead-generation programs, candidates with unresolved location or schedule conflicts, and prior-issue events only after their verified end time had passed. Eventbrite rate-limited direct verification of the Quantum Biology ticket page, so that listing relies on its active organizer Meetup page; the SoCal Python source also returned an error during this sweep. Event details and availability can still change—check the linked registration page before traveling.
