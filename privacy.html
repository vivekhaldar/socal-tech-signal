SoCal / Signal

Plain-English privacy

Your inbox is not inventory.

SoCal Tech Signal collects your email address only when you ask to receive the weekly event list. A confirmation email is required before you are subscribed.

What is collected

Your email address, confirmation status, subscription preferences, and the operational delivery events needed to handle bounces, complaints, and unsubscribes.

How it is used

To send the weekly SoCal Tech Signal and essential subscription messages. The list is not sold or rented.

Service provider

Resend processes subscriber and delivery data on behalf of SoCal Tech Signal. Its unsubscribe and suppression systems are used to respect your choices and protect deliverability.

Your choices

Every issue includes an unsubscribe link. You may also request access to or deletion of your subscriber record by emailing vh@vivekhaldar.com.
